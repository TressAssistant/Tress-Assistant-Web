Here is how you can interact with the 3D model:

**On Desktop:**
- **Rotate:** Click and drag with your mouse to rotate the model and view it from different angles.
- **Zoom:** Use your mouse scroll wheel to zoom in and out for a closer or wider view.
- **Pan:** Hold the right mouse button (or Shift + left mouse button) and drag to move the model within the frame.

**On Mobile:**
- **Rotate:** Use one finger to drag and rotate the model.
- **Zoom:** Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out.
- **Pan:** Use two fingers to drag and move the model around the screen.
